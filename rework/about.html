About MoonDust

MoonDust began in 2016 by LittlePotato and shortly later with the magnificent frogboy.
At the time MoonDust's playerbase mostly consisted of LittlePotato's dumb 5th grade class mates.

These days, MoonDust is a cozy community of silly Minecraft players.
We're all here to have a good time and not take things too seriously :3
